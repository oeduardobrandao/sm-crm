# Leads Management Specification

## Purpose
Tracks prospective clients through a qualification pipeline with source tracking and categorization.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Lead CRUD
The system SHALL support lead records with fields: name, email, Instagram handle, source channel, specialty, revenue range, tags, notes, and status.

### Requirement: Lead Statuses
The system SHALL track lead statuses: New, Contacted, Qualified, Lost, and Converted.

### Requirement: Source Channels
The system SHALL categorize leads by source: Instagram, Facebook, Google Ads, Referral, Website, WhatsApp, Typeform, and Other.

### Requirement: Revenue Range Classification
The system SHALL classify leads by revenue range for qualification purposes.

### Requirement: Search and Sort
The system SHALL allow searching and sorting leads by multiple columns.

### Requirement: CSV Import
The system SHALL allow bulk lead creation via CSV file upload.
