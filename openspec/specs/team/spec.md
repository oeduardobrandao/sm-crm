# Team Management Specification

## Purpose
Manages team member records, employment types, costs, and role-based organization.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Team Member CRUD
The system SHALL support team member records with fields: name, role/position, employment type, monthly cost, and payment day.

### Requirement: Employment Types
The system SHALL support employment types: CLT, Monthly Freelancer, and On-Demand Freelancer.

### Requirement: Cost Aggregation
The system SHALL aggregate and display total team costs with role-based filtering.

### Requirement: Member Detail Pages
The system SHALL provide detail pages for each team member with history and metrics.

### Requirement: Member Avatars
The system SHALL display member avatars with color-coded indicators.

### Requirement: CSV Import
The system SHALL allow bulk team member creation via CSV file upload.
